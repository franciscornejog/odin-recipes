Birria

Description

This Mexican recipe is delicious yet difficult to make.

Ingredients

  • 4 guajillo
  • 1/2 onion
  • 8 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 2lb tomatoes

Steps

  1. Trim excess fat off meat if any
  2. Soak the meat for 2 to 4 hours
  3. Prep chile paste an hour before meat is ready
    1. Separate seeds and skins
    2. Rinse and boil skins to soften them
    3. Roast seeds carefully, avoid black
  4. Drain meat
  5. Dip meat into sauce
